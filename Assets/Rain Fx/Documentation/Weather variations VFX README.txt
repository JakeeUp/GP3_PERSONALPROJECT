-Drag and drop the cloud and the rain and other weather prefabs onto the Hieraricy.
-The height can be adjusted by changing the y Transform position of your particle System.
-In order to Increase or decrease the area over which your weather element should spawn, 
 Go to Shape in the Inepctor of Particle System and change the scale of x and y according to your desire.
-You can also increase or decrease the amount of Snow or clouds being spawned at a time,
 by going to Inspector Emission, and change the value of Rate over time.
